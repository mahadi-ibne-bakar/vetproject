pets Our Story

Making vet care accessible
for every pet in Bangladesh

Amarvet was born out of a simple observation — too many pet owners in Bangladesh struggle to access qualified veterinary advice. Clinics are far, queues are long, and taking a stressed cat or dog out is difficult. We built the solution.

format_quote

Our mission is to connect every pet owner in Bangladesh with a certified veterinarian — from anywhere, at any time, affordably.

info What We Do

Amarvet is an online veterinary consultation platform built specifically for Bangladesh. We connect cat and dog owners with certified, BVC-registered veterinarians via Google Meet video calls.

From diagnosing early symptoms to giving dietary advice, our vets provide professional guidance that helps you make the right decisions for your pet's health — without leaving your home.
